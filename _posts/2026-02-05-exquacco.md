This interdisciplinary school brings together formal, experimental, field & computational linguists, with hands-on workshops on quantitative methods, NLP, corpus annotation & more. I’ll be teaching “A statistical toolkit for typology”, with practical R exercises on cross-linguistic data. The topics are as follows: phylogenetic regression, Multidimensional Scaling, conditional inference trees & random forests.
